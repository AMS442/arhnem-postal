Arnhem Postal History

Welcome to the Arnhem Postal History Project, a collection of stamps, covers, correspondence, photographs and documents from the Netherlands during World War II. Through the lens of postal services &emd; including postmarks, censor markings, and routing &emd; the collection offers a glimpse of everyday life in tumultuous times. The project includes a database of images, people and places represented by the objects in the collection; exhibits providing more in depth views of time periods and events; and short essays focused items of interest.
